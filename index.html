Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

At Kohat Hujra Restaurant, we bring the rich heritage of Pashtun hospitality to your table. Our name "Hujra" represents the traditional gathering place where guests are honored with the finest food and warmest welcome.

Every dish we serve is a celebration of authentic Shinwari and Pashtun flavors, prepared using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. From our signature Mutton Karahi to our succulent Chicken Sajji, each meal is crafted with passion and precision.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open daily from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. We welcome dine-in, takeaway, and delivery orders throughout our operating hours.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! You can order via WhatsApp at +92 333 9665538 and we'll deliver fresh, hot food to your doorstep in Kohat area.

Can I book the restaurant for events?

Absolutely! We cater to family gatherings, corporate events, and special occasions. Contact us on WhatsApp or call us to discuss your requirements.

What makes your food authentic Shinwari?

We use traditional Pashtun recipes, premium quality meat, and authentic cooking methods passed down through generations. Our chefs specialize in Shinwari cuisine.

Do you have vegetarian options?

While we specialize in meat dishes, we offer fresh salads, raita, and can prepare vegetable sides upon request. Please contact us for specific requirements.

How do I place a WhatsApp order?

Simply click "Order Now" on any dish, select quantity, and it will open WhatsApp with your order details. You can then send it directly to us!
