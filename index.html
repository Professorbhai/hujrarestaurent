Experience Traditional Pashtun Hospitality

At Kohat Hujra Restaurant, we bring you the finest authentic Shinwari and Pashtun cuisine, prepared with passion and served with the warmth of traditional hospitality. Every dish tells a story of our rich cultural heritage.

From our signature Mutton Karahi to the exquisite Dum Pukht, each recipe has been perfected over generations. We use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure an unforgettable dining experience.
